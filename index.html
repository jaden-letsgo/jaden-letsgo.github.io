My Favorite breed

There are many different dog breeds in the world, but if you ask me what is my favorite dog breed, I would say that Border Collie is the only one!
And here's a unordered list of reasons that I think Border Collie is the best dog breed in the world:

  • Smart
  • Hard Working
  • Active
  • Easy to care

If you would like to have a lovely friend, or family depends on your opinion, I will definitely suggest you go check out your local animal shelters to see if there is one for you!
